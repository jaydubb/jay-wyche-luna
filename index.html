About

I was born and raised in Boston, MA, and now call Durham, NC home. With over a decade of experience as a product and UX designer, I have worked across enterprise and SaaS environments to create intuitive, scalable solutions in data governance, security, and analytics. My work often sits at the intersection of design systems, product strategy, and user research, where I help teams bring clarity and coherence to complex workflows.

Beyond my professional work, I find inspiration in creative and cultural pursuits. I enjoy photography, painting, and vibing to old school hip-hop and R&B. These interests keep me balanced and fuel my creativity and perspective as a designer.
